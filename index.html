INTRODUCING RV400 BRZ

Introducing the RV400 BRZ - a result of our in-depth study into the needs and preferences of riders who are motoring enthusiasts. We recognize that while technology can enhance the riding experience, not every rider requires an extensive array of features. Our commitment to putting riders first led us to create the RV400 BRZ, addressing the gap in the market for those seeking an affordable yet exhilarating biking experience. With a vibrant palette of five colours including Lunar Green, Pacific Blue, Dark Silver, Rebel Red, and Cosmic Black, this model isn't just a motorcycle: it's a bold statement.


To make electric bikes accessible to all segment of riders, we carefully considered the features that truly matter. The RV400 BRZ incorporates essential elements, such as a mechanical key to start the bike, ensuring a seamless and user-friendly experience for the riders who crave simplicity without sacrificing performance.

RV400 - LIGHTNING YELLOW

Introducing the Lightning Yellow variant for the RV400 - a stunning blend of vibrancy and athleticism. This vivid hue ignites a new chapter in electric biking, infusing your ride with an unmistakable sense of energy and style.


The Lightning Yellow RV400 stands out with its glossy finish, creating a captivating dual-tone effect alongside the blacked-out underbody, lower fairing, and wheels. This striking contrast accentuates the bike's sleek silhouette, ensuring a bold and memorable presence on the road.


Experience the perfect fusion of sporty sophistication and contemporary design with the RV400 in Lightning Yellow. Elevate any journey and make a vibrant statement while enjoying an electrifying ride with style and great performance.
